# Chocolate Cake 

**Description:**  
A rich and moist chocolate cake for all occasions.

## Ingredients:
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup cocoa powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs

## Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
2. Mix dry ingredients.
3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
4. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
5. Let it cool and enjoy!
