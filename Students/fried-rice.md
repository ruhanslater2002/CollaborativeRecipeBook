# Fried Rice

## Ingredients
- 3 cups Cooked Rice  
- 3 Eggs  
- 1 cup Diced Vegetables  
- 2 tbsp Olive Oil  
- 1/3 cup Soy Sauce  
- 1/3 cup Water

## Instructions
1. Saut√© vegetables in oil.
2. Add rice and water, stir.
3. Scramble eggs separately and mix in.
4. Add soy sauce and serve.
